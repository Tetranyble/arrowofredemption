A BRIEF BIOGRAPHY OF THE PASTOR (ARM)”BENSON IYAYE”

Benson Iyaye is the founder and senior pastor of Arrow of Redemption Ministries, Inc. The Evangelist popularly known as “the preacher of holiness” is a year older today. The man of God is seventy today!

1940
MAY 1ST

EARLY DAYS:

Evangelist Benson Iyaye was born on the 9th of September, 1952 in Ugbege, Warri South Local Government Area of Delta State Nigeria. He was born into the family of late Mr. Mark Toghanranro from the AdenoilaIkoloma and Omoregha of Ilaje Local Government Area of Ondo State and Late Madam TitiOkoro also from Abare and Osubi Both in Delta State of Nigeria.

1952
SEPTEMBER 9TH

Pastor's Education

He got his Primary Education Certificate in Municipan Council School, Amigbo, Port Harcourt in 1973 While his Secondary Education was between 1947-1979 at Lagos Baptist Secondary School, Orile-Agege, Where he got his West Africa School Certificate in 1978. While In Baptist School, he got born again in the assembly ground under the ministration of Brother Daniel Ifeagwu (now Dr. Daniel) of Heaven Gate Ministries.

1997
JUN 1

Return to Warri

Benson returned to warri, joined the church of God Mission, Odibo Branch. As the burden for souls increased, God led him to work under Jesus Christ for the Masses Ministries. There he opened branches in Owhase, D.S.C. Township and Aladja all in Udu area. In 1987, he continued ministerial work till he was ordained as Evangelist at the Living Truth Church 30th August, 1987, Under the leadership of Bishop Cletus Otabon now the presiding Bishop of the Sanctuary of the Saints Church in Warri.

The presiding Bishop through divine revelation recognized the gift and uniqueness of God in Evangelist Benson and encouraged him to separate himself for a higher calling in Ministry.

1987
AUGUST 30TH

Conception

By May 27th 1990, he founded the Arrow of Redemption Ministries. He attended Rivers Bible College in Eleme Port Harcourt 1990-1992 for his Diploma and the Nigeria Advanced School of Theology Ewu-Edo State for his bachelor of Art in Christian Education 2000 to 2002.

He is also an Alumnus at Haggai Institute of Advanced Leadership Training Skill. He is currently involved in mission within and outside the Nation of Nigeria with great and proving results, touching lives and raising leaders for the end-time Gospel work.

Behind every successful minister of God is a praying wife. He got married to Blessing Iyaye on December 12th, 1987 and God has blessed the marriage with six children and grandchildren.

1990
MAY 27TH
